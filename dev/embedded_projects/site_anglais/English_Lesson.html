Try to not laugh !

    The Japanese are in constant search for new games to subject their peers and what better place for children like them as a classroom ? The principle ? Watch a video of a person (in this case, a comedian) in a vain attempt to speak English. If you laugh anyway, you are punished and the classic risk punishment in the Japanese games, a stick. Although at first the shots are nice, as and when the video shots are becoming stronger. Nothing hilarious well you tell me, but all things considered, I will challenge you not to laugh too.

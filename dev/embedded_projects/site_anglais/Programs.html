    Gaki no Tsukai is been created in 1989 by Hitoshi Matsumoto and Masatoshi Hamada, very famous famous comedians in japan. They reach the 1000 th episode in 2010, and they are still on Nippon TV.

There is sixteen segments in this show :


    The bigger is the Batsu Games ( the punishments games ). It’s games organized when a cast member loses a bet. The loser has to accomplish a pledge, like a physical game. But there are a lot of different batsu games. Between 1990 and 2003, at least 20 batsu games were accomplished by the cast.

    The kiki series is a blind test where the players have to guess what kinds of food they are eating. the winner receive 100 000 yen ( 773.14 € ), and the losers are punished.

    In the 500 Quizzes , famous japanese people are questioned with basic question, like "What is your favourite?" or "Which is the most interesting?". The players have to anticipate the answers. The winner win a prize, and obviously, the loser is punished.

    The Cosplay Bus Tour series start with mini games. All the participant are playing dressed with cosplay cloth. If a player lose 3 times, he is filed in the downtown. He have to buy something and get back afoot to the studio.

    The Absolutely Tasty Series is a cooking game. The players have ingredients and they have to prepare foods. Most of the time, the result is inedible.

    But if the batsu games are for the cast, all the others games punish losers. It’s most of the time a physical punishment, like be hit with a book, or even slapped in the balls.

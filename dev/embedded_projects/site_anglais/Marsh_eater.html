    Marshmallow eater





It becomes weird ..

     There is a game known worldwide where you are tied at the waist with an elastic, and you have to touch a wall. Of course, the closer you get, the more the pressure is high, and you’re often brought back by the elastic to the start. This japan tv game is a mix between this game, and the expression “carrot or the stick”. The aim is simple. There is two teams. Each their turn, there will be tied with an elastic, and they have to catch without their hands, so with their mouth, a marshmallow. To make the game more complicated, there is no elastic tied on their waist, but several elastics around their heads. Of course, their faces are deformed when the elastics pulls, so everyone who play at this game looks like a complete dumbass. Oh, and by the way, it’s a Gaki no Tsukai’s game, so if you’re team didn’t catch the carrot, you will feel the stick…

    Silent Library




Be silent, it's a library !

    Here we are again, gukai no tsukai shows us a new game called Gukai no Tsukai - Silent Library. This popular comedy/variety series was hosted by the popular Japanese comedy duo Downtown (Hitoshi Matsumoto and Masatoshi Hamada). It featured segments called "Batsu" (roughly translated to "Torture!" Is it any wonder a US network grabbed this one?) During each Batsu, there's a task or question that must be completed. Here the actors are in a library where they are supposed to draw a card and if their is a card with a death skull the made the challenge who is write on a paper. The same guy is like the victim of the game, indeed he draw all the cards who are with a dead skull which is pretty funny. You want to know who is going to win ? nobody’s going to win !! it’s just a game for fun with challenges which will make you have a good time.

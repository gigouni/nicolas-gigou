    Konoichi




Run for your life !

    Have you ever seen Total Wipeout ? No ? Kunoichi was a women's obstacle course competition held in Japan, it is a spin-off of Sasuke, another obstacle course series. Kunoichi is different from Sasuke because the competitors are exclusively female. Each competition starts with 100 competitors from different backgrounds such as actors, comedians, housewives, athletes, amateur and Olympians. This competition is like Total Wipeout a French Adaptation, this competition consists to win the race against the competitors. In the race, women competitors have to overcome obstacles and won the race with the smallest time they can.
